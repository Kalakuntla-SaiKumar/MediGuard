Understanding Your Medication

How to Use Medicines Safely

Always follow prescribed dosage instructions and never mix medications without checking interactions. MediGuard helps you verify drug safety, identify risks, and understand how medicines affect your body. Proper usage reduces side effects and improves treatment effectiveness.

Why Drug Knowledge Matters

Knowing how your medication works helps prevent overdose, allergic reactions, and dangerous combinations. MediGuard provides reliable safety information so patients and healthcare professionals can make confident decisions.
